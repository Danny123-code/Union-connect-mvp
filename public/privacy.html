Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026
This is a general-purpose template, not a legal document reviewed by an attorney. Before relying on it for a real, paying-customer launch, have a lawyer review and adapt it to your business and jurisdiction (e.g. GDPR/CCPA obligations if you'll have EU or California users).

1. What we collect

When you create a workspace, we collect the information you provide directly: your name, email address, company name, industry, and the vendors, operations, messages, and documents you create or upload while using the Service.

2. How we use it

  • To operate your workspace and keep your company's data private from other companies
  • To authenticate you (sessions, password hashing)
  • To send transactional emails, like a welcome message when you sign up or a password reset link when you request one
  • To respond when you contact us for support

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your uploaded documents or messages for advertising.

3. How we store it

Data is stored on our hosting provider's infrastructure. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they're hashed using a salted, one-way algorithm (scrypt). Uploaded files are stored on a private server volume, scoped to your company's workspace.

4. Email

We use a third-party transactional email provider (currently Resend) to send welcome emails and password reset links. Your email address is shared with that provider only for the purpose of delivering those messages.

5. Data retention and deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you'd like your account and associated data deleted, contact us and we'll process the request.

6. Cookies

We use a single essential cookie to keep you logged in (an HttpOnly session cookie). We don't use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Your rights

You can ask us what data we hold about you, request a correction, or request deletion at any time by contacting us.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us at daniellob890@gmail.com.
